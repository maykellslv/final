About us

We are a family-owned and operated catering company based in Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines. Since 1997, we have evolved from a small pizzeria to a restaurant, a canteen concessionaire, and now a full-service catering company. Our specialty lies in delivering minimalist elegance and comforting cuisine. Our chefs have perfected a menu of tried-and-true dishes designed to delight you and your guests, ensuring a joyful and satisfying dining experience.
